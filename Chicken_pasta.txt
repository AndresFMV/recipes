# Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A creamy, cheesy pasta dish that's easy to make and absolutely delicious.

## Ingredients
- 200g fettuccine pasta
- 2 chicken breasts, sliced
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Chopped parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions.
2. While the pasta is cooking, heat some oil in a pan and add the chicken. Cook until it's no longer pink.
3. Add the garlic to the pan and cook for another minute.
4. Add the heavy cream, Parmesan, salt, and pepper to the pan. Stir well and simmer for about 5 minutes.
5. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the sauce. Stir well to combine.
6. Serve hot with some chopped parsley on top.

Enjoy your meal!!
