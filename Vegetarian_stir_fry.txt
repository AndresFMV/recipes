# Vegetarian Stir Fry

A quick, healthy, and flavorful dish packed with colorful veggies.

## Ingredients
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 100g broccoli florets
- 100g snap peas
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat some oil in a wok or large frying pan.
2. Add the vegetables to the pan and stir fry for about 5 minutes, until they're tender but still crisp.
3. Add the garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper to the pan. Stir well to combine
