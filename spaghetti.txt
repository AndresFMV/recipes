# Spaghetti Bolognese

This is a traditional Italian dish that I love. It's hearty, delicious, and perfect for a family dinner!

## Ingredients
- 200g spaghetti
- 400g minced beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 400g tin chopped tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat some oil in a pan and add the onion and garlic. Cook until they are soft.
3. Add the minced beef to the pan and cook until it's browned.
4. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, and pepper to the pan. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the sauce. Stir well to combine.
6. Serve hot with some grated Parmesan cheese on top.

## Tips and Notes
- You can add some red wine to the sauce for extra flavor.
- This dish is best served with a side of garlic bread.

Enjoy your meal!
